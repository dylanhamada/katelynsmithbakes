About me

I’m Katelyn, and I love to bake! Been doing it since I was yay big, and I don’t see myself stopping any time soon. My cookies, cakes, and pastries have been described as “transcendent,” “unreal,” and “OMG.” I have a great idea: I should cater your next event!

By day, I’m a mild-mannered Quabidy Assurance specialist. By night... I’m also mild-mannered, but with pyjamas on! Did I mention I love to bake? You can often find me with a whisk and mixing bowl in hand.

The biggest thing for me is putting smiles on the faces of everyone who gets to sample my goodies. If my baking skill was a D&D character stat, I’d probably be maxed at 20. Extra points if you know what I'm talking about.

I believe there’s a baked confection for every special occasion. Let me help you find yours!
